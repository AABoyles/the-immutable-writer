The Immutable Writer

A Typewriter Emulator

Because Perfect is the Enemy of Productive

Have you ever wanted to just write, but gotten wrapped up in formatting, spelling, grammar? If you'd been typing on a typewriter, you'd simply have to accept what you'd already typed without correction. The Immutable Writer is a writing environment designed to emulate that finality.

You cannot edit. You cannot cut and paste. You cannot go back. It's like every character gets punched into the page the moment you type it.

So just relax! Type a first draft, download it, and then open it up in normal text editor to revise it.
